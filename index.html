Humanitarian

From Yemen to Syria and to Congo, the people of these countries need our help. Today, Yemen is facing the worst humanitarian crisis in the world since 1945. Millions are dying due to war, starvation or sickness. With your help, these charities will make sure your donation will be put to good use.

Children

Many kids around the world are less fortunate and get no type of education whatsoever. Other kids, including in our own neighborhoods, go to sleep hungry. Some just need a safe place to stay after school or on weekends instead of on the streets. We want every child around the world safe & happy.

Civil Rights

The charities provided will tackle specific problems that people of color face everyday and try to help solve them or make them better. Most of these top rated charities focus on the youth


Our Goal

Our website, OneHeart, provides people with trustable charities and foundations. We wish to create a platform to have people feel secure about donating money and feeling confident that their donations will be put to good-use! This will help maximize the effectiveness of every dollar contributed to charity by providing donors with the information they need to make more informed giving decisions.Our goal is to provide accurate information on charities based on our own research and provide our users a gateway to each foundation.

About Us

We, the creators of OneHeart, made it up to us to provide the people with what we believe to be the most important causes and the top charities that you, as a donor, can trust. We've all seen commercials and articles about charities "doing amazing work" when in reality they're using your money for the wrong reasons. We've done our very own research about each charity and we've unoficially partnered with Charity Watchdogs and Charity Navigator to bring you only the best of the best.
